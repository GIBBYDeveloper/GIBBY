To make a weapon show the name in your Weapon Wheel, this is what you need!

Say you wanted a gun while the spawncode "EXAMPLEPISTOL" but you wanted it to say "Cool Pistol" In the weapon wheel.

weaponNames["WT_EXAMPLEPISTOL"] = "Cool Pistol"


             ^SPAWNCODE^          ^WHAT IT SHOULD 
                                   SHOW IN WEAPON 
                                   WHEEL^
